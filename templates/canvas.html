Business Model Canvas
Canvas is a strategic management tool used by businesses to describe, design, challenge, and invent business models. It provides a visual framework for brainstorming and analyzing various aspects of a business model in a concise and structured manner. The canvas consists of nine key building blocks that represent the critical components of a business model:
1. Key partners are the companies or people your business works with to create a strategic relationship.
2. Key Activities are specific activities or tasks that are fundamental to the operation of your business.
3. Key Resources are the assets necessary to operate and deliver your value proposition.
4. Value Propositions:are arguably the most important element of the business model canvas template. The value proposition determines the fundamental offering the company is trying to give its customers.
5. Customer Relationships are the different types of interactions a company has with its customers.
6. Channels are the different structures and methods that are used to deliver your company’s product and value proposition to its customers.
7. Customer Segments are the different types of customers that a company manages. A company that produces different products will need to interact with different types of customers.
8. Cost Structure refers to how a company spends money on operations. It consists of the company’s key costs and the company’s level of focus on costs.
9. Revenue Streams are the different ways your company’s value proposition generates money.
